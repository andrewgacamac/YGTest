Go-to-Market Strategy

05

The neighbourhood Takeover Method

Instead of broad marketing, we dominate one neighbourhood at a time—creating visible social proof that spreads organically.

Step 1

Secure Anchor Installation

Offer 20% discount to first customer in target neighbourhood (Lorne Park, Port Credit, The Kingsway) in exchange for yard sign, testimonial, and hosting a backyard tour event.

Step 2

neighbourhood Blitz

Door-knock 50 homes within 500m radius. Script: "Your neighbour at [address] just transformed their backyard. We're offering neighbours a preview this Saturday plus 10% off."

Step 3

Backyard Tour Event

Host 2-hour open house at completed project. Bring iPad with 3D design software. Collect contact info. Show what's possible.

Step 4

Convert & Repeat

Target: Convert 2-3 neighbours within 60 days. Each new installation becomes another anchor. Repeat the cycle. Dominate the area.
