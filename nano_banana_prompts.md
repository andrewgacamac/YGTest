# Nano Banana Image Prompts for YardGuard

Use these prompts to generate high-quality, realistic images for the YardGuard website.

## Problem Carousel Images (8 Cards)

**Style Note:** Photorealistic, high resolution, slightly desaturated or cooler tones to emphasize the "problem" state, but not overly depressing. Cinematic lighting. 4:3 or 1:1 aspect ratio.

### 1. The Muddy Mess
**Prompt:**
> Close-up shot of a modern backdoor interior entryway with muddy paw prints tracked across a clean hardwood floor. In the background through the glass door, a wet, muddy backyard is visible with a golden retriever looking guilty. High detailed texture of the mud.

### 2. Yellow Dog Spots
**Prompt:**
> Eye-level shot of a suburban backyard lawn that is patchy with distinct yellow and brown urine burn spots from a dog. A dog ball lies in the grass. The grass looks otherwise cared for but ruined by the spots. Realistic suburban fence in background.

### 3. Endless Mowing
**Prompt:**
> Low angle shot of a gas lawnmower sitting in tall, overgrown grass. Heat waves visible rising from the engine. A homeowner's hand is wiping sweat from their forehead in the foreground (blurred). The scene conveys heat, noise, and exhaustion. Summer afternoon lighting.

### 4. Sky-High Water Bills
**Prompt:**
> Close-up focused on a residential water meter or a sprinkler head gushing water. In the foreground, a person holds a paper water bill with a shocked expression (focus on the bill/hands). The background is a lush green lawn being watered by multiple sprinklers.

### 5. Constant Weeding
**Prompt:**
> Close-up, ground-level perspective of hands wearing gardening gloves pulling stubborn dandelions from a lawn. The weeds are deep-rooted. The grass is interspersed with various weeds (clover, crabgrass). The soil looks dry and hard.

### 6. Physical Strain (Aging/Back Pain)
**Prompt:**
> Medium shot of an older homeowner (60s) clutching their lower back in pain while standing next to a rake and a pile of yard waste in a backyard. The expression is one of discomfort. Sunlight is harsh. Emphasize the physical toll of yard work.

### 7. New Build Dirt Pit
**Prompt:**
> Wide shot of a newly built suburban home (modern architecture) with a backyard that is completely barren dirt and mud. No grass, just construction debris and tire tracks. A "sold" sign might be visible in the distance. The contrast between the new house and the ugly yard is key.

### 8. The View from Inside (WFH Window)
**Prompt:**
> View looking *out* through a modern living room window. The interior is cozy and clean (coffee cup on sill), but the view through the glass is of a neglect, patchy, ugly backyard. Rain streaks on the window. The contrast between the sanctuary inside and the mess outside.

## Hero Images (Before & After)

**Style Note:** These need to be perfectly matched angles if possible, or visually distinct enough to represent the transformation. Wide angle, aspirational, high definition. 16:9 aspect ratio.

### Hero Before
**Prompt:**
> Wide angle shot of a typical suburban backyard in Toronto/GTA. The grass is patchy, there's a bald spot under a tree, maybe a plastic chair overturned. The fence looks weathered. Overcast lighting. The yard looks "okay" but uninspiring and high-maintenance.

### Hero After
**Prompt:**
> Same angle wide shot of the same suburban backyard transformed with diverse premium artificial turf. Perfectly green and manicured. A defined stone patio edge. A lounge chair with a colorful cushion. Warm golden hour sunlight. A happy dog is lying on the grass. The fence looks stained and new. The vibe is "clean, low-maintenance luxury."

## Service Area Map

**Style Note:** Clean, professional 2D map in a modern tech/startup style. Light grayscale background map of the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) with the specific service areas highlighted in a soft, trust-inspiring green (#4CAF50).

### Service Map
**Prompt:**
> Professional flat design map of the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) focusing on the west end. Highlight the regions of Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, and Oakville in a semi-transparent bright green overlay. The rest of the map (Toronto, Vaughn, etc.) should be in subtle grayscale. Clear, legible labels for "Etobicoke", "Mississauga", "Oakville", "Brampton". White background, clean lines, high resolution.
