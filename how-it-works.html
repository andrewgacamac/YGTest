Common Questions

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects are completed in 2-5 days, depending on size and complexity. Larger projects or those with significant hardscaping may take longer.

Is the design visualization really free?

Yes! The design visualization is completely free when you book with us. If you decide not to proceed, there's no charge - we want you to see exactly what you're getting before committing.

What happens to my existing lawn?

We remove all existing grass, weeds, and debris as part of the installation process. Everything is hauled away - you don't need to worry about disposal.

Do I need to be home during installation?

Not necessarily. Once we have access to the work area, our crew can complete the installation. We'll coordinate with you for the initial setup and final walkthrough.
