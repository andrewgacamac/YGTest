# Residential Artificial Turf Strategy
## Yard Guard Landscaping - Focused Go-to-Market Plan

> **Focus:** Residential market only
> **Goal:** Establish market position and beat established competitors
> **Timeline:** 12-month execution plan
> **Budget:** $15,000 - $25,000 total investment

## Strategic Foundation

### Why Yard Guard Will Win

| Competitor Weakness | Yard Guard Advantage |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| Turf-only companies | Full backyard integration (turf + patio + features) |
| No visualization tools | 3D design shows clients their yard BEFORE building |
| Seasonal relationship | Year-round engagement via snow removal |

**Not This:** "We install artificial turf"

**This:** "We transform backyards into low-maintenance outdoor living spaces"

### 1.3 Pricing Structure

**Base Pricing (Residential):**

| Project Type | Price/Sq Ft | Min Project |
|--------------|-------------|-------------|
| Basic lawn replacement | $12-15 | $4,000 |
| Premium lawn (thicker pile) | $15-18 | $5,000 |
| Pet-friendly turf | $14-17 | $4,500 |
| Putting green | $22-28 | $6,000 |
| Pool surround | $14-17 | $5,000 |

**Pricing Formula:**
```
Materials (40%) + Labour (30%) + Overhead (15%) + Profit (15%) = Price
```

**Example 500 sq ft project:**
- Materials: $2,000 (turf + base + infill)
- Labour: $1,500 (2 crew, 1.5 days)
- Overhead: $750
- Profit: $750
- **Total: $5,000 ($10/sq ft)**

### 1.5 The 3D Advantage Setup

**This is your #1 competitive weapon.**

**Software Options:**

| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **iScape** | $30/month | Quick visualizations, mobile |
| **Cedreo** | $99/month | Professional 3D renders |
| **PRO Landscape** | $1,500 one-time | Full design suite |

**Recommendation:** Start with **iScape** ($30/month) - easy to learn, impressive results

**The Sales Process:**

```
1. Site visit + photos (Free)
         ↓
2. Create 3D visualization (1-2 hours)
         ↓
3. Present design to client
   "Here's YOUR backyard with turf installed"
         ↓
4. Client signs (higher close rate)
```

**Why This Beats Competition:**
- Competitors show generic photos
- You show THEIR yard transformed
- Removes buyer uncertainty
- Justifies premium pricing

**Timeline:** Week 3-4
**Cost:** $30/month ($360/year)

## Phase 3: Market Penetration (Months 4-8)

### 3.1 The Neighborhood Strategy

**Concept:** Dominate one neighborhood before expanding

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Select Target Neighborhood**

   Priority areas in Yard Guard's territory:
   | Area | Avg Home Value | Why Target |
   |------|----------------|------------|
   | Lorne Park | $1.5M+ | Large lots, affluent |
   | Port Credit | $1.2M+ | Established, visible yards |
   | Erindale | $800K+ | Family-focused |
   | The Kingsway (Etobicoke) | $2M+ | Premium market |

2. **Complete First Installation**
   - Use Founding Customer in target area
   - Install prominent yard sign
   - Get permission for "open house" tour

3. **Neighborhood Blitz (Week of Completion)**
   - Door-knock 50 homes within 500m
   - Script: "Your neighbor at [address] just had their backyard transformed. We're offering neighbors a preview this Saturday plus 10% off if you book this month."
   - Leave door hanger with before/after photo

4. **Host "Backyard Tour" Event**
   - Saturday afternoon, 2-hour window
   - Invite neighbors to see completed project
   - Have iPad ready with 3D designs
   - Collect contact info for follow-up

5. **Follow-Up Sequence**
   - Day 3: Email photos of completed project
   - Day 7: Call to offer free 3D mockup
   - Day 14: Final "neighborhood pricing expires" message

**Expected Results:**
- 50 doors knocked → 10-15 conversations
- Backyard tour → 5-10 attendees
- Conversions within 60 days → 2-3 projects

**Cost:** $200 (door hangers, refreshments for tour)

## Phase 4: Scale & Differentiate (Months 8-12)

### 4.1 The "See It First" Guarantee

**Once you have portfolio + process dialed in, launch this:**

> **"See Your Backyard Transformed in 3D Before You Commit - Guaranteed"**
>
> We'll create a photorealistic 3D design of your specific backyard with artificial turf installed. If you don't love it, you pay nothing. No other turf company offers this.

**How It Works:**
1. Free site visit + measurements
2. 3D design created (1-2 hours your time)
3. Presentation meeting with client
4. If they proceed → design fee waived
5. If they don't → charge $150 design fee (covers your time)

**This beats every competitor** because:
- They show generic photos
- You show the customer's ACTUAL yard
- Removes #1 barrier: "I can't visualize it"

---

### 4.2 The Maintenance Program

**Turf needs minimal maintenance, but "minimal" isn't "zero."**

**Annual Maintenance Package: $299/year**

Includes:
- Spring inspection and debris removal
- Infill level check and top-up if needed
- Deep cleaning/rinse
- Brush fibers to restore appearance
- Check seams and edges
- Written condition report

**Why Offer This:**
1. Recurring revenue ($299 × 50 clients = $14,950/year)
2. Ongoing relationship (referrals)
3. Upsell opportunity (they see new services)
4. Protects your reputation (turf stays looking good)

---
