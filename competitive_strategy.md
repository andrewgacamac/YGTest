# BATTLEGROUND 4: Create Innovative Business Models

## Strategy 4.1: The "Neighborhood Takeover" Strategy

### The Concept

Instead of marketing broadly, **dominate one neighborhood at a time.**

### How It Works

1. **Identify target neighborhood:**
   - High income ($150K+ household)
   - Larger lots (5,000+ sq ft)
   - Established area (20+ year old homes)
   - Visible front/back yards

2. **Secure first installation:**
   - Offer 15-20% discount
   - In exchange for: yard sign, testimonial, hosting tour

3. **Launch "Neighborhood Blitz":**
   - Door-to-door within 500m radius
   - "Your neighbor at [address] just transformed their backyard..."
   - Invite to see the completed project
   - Limited-time neighborhood pricing

4. **Create social proof cascade:**
   - First installation leads to curious neighbors
   - Offer "neighbor discount" for adjacent properties
   - Each new install = more visibility
