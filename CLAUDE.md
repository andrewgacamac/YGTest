## Strategic Direction

### Key Differentiators to Develop
1. **"Complete Backyard Transformation"** - Not just turf, but full outdoor living
2. **3D Visualization** - Show clients their backyard before building
3. **Pet Turf Specialization** - "PawGuard" sub-brand opportunity
4. **Golf Green Premium** - "GreenGuard" sub-brand for affluent golfers
5. **Year-Round Service** - Leverage snow removal for ongoing relationships

### Target Market Segments
- Residential homeowners (Mississauga, Etobicoke, GTA)
- Pet owners with damaged lawns
- Golf enthusiasts wanting practice greens
- Commercial properties (daycares, condos, restaurants)

## When Working on This Project

- Reference existing documents before creating new analysis
- Consider Canadian climate factors (winter, freeze-thaw cycles)
- Focus on GTA market specifically
- Compare against identified competitors (SynTurf, Echoing Green, Design Turf, etc.)
- Keep recommendations actionable and budget-conscious for a small business
- Pricing should be in CAD
